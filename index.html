Campus Life

school playground

Was established in 2014

The pre-primary kids are more often here. They love to swing. Sometimes, all the rides are empty but kids are making lines just for the swing! We like it here. If our bus gets delayed or our parents haven't come, we can use this. Students who have graduated can also come here to see their school. It is a nice place to have fun or to talk to your friends about doubts. Even teachers come here to sit and revise what they will teach. In summer time, many students come here to shout and say to the school building, "See you after a month!" It is so loud, even the pigeons near our ground fly away. On Makar Sakranti, you can come here to prepare your kites for the kite-flying event which happens every year in our school.

basketball court

Was established in 2016

This is an interesting place to which all the students like to go. We play basketball here. Higher-grade students learn to spin balls and move them swiftly. We learn to build our stamina to quickly pass the ball to somebody else. The pre-primary students run as fast as they can and learn to use hula-hoops. It's a very lively place. It is active almost everyday, except weekends of course.It is a nice change of scenery from the classrooms You can also see the sunrise there, which is if you wake up at the school at 5:35!! ;P

school auditorium

Was established in 2015

Our school auditorium is place where we go once in a week. It is a place where the principal sir tells us about the upcoming events and/or holidays. Many challenges are also conducted in the auditorium too, like the Underwater GK quiz which happened last year. They also show videos to pre-primary kids on the projector about life-lessons or why festivals are celebrated. Medals are also given to higher-students here. We also have dance celebrations and competitions there. Feels good to dance and sing on some beats there! Laboratory students are given awards for their sincerity here also.

    What's New

  • Book Submission

    Our school has recently found a new way for us to submit our books. We have to write the uploaded notes and a parent of ours has to go to the school to drop it so that it can be officially stamped. Incase our parents are out of station we can send PDFs of the child's books.

    • Student Appreciation

      Appreciation is something all of us like. So, SESS awards a student from each class who is the following: *100% Attendance * Wearing school uniform for lectures * Punctuality * Self-disciplined * Diligent * Active participation in classroom and co-curricular activities. This is a really great way to encourage the students to follow these prompts.

    Madhuri Dixit- Indian Actress

    Madhuri Dixit


    Madhuri Dixit was born on 15 May 1967 into a Marathi Kokanastha Brahmin family in Bombay (present-day Mumbai) to Shankar and Snehlata Dixit. She has two elder sisters and an elder brother. She kindled an interest in dance at an early age of three, and went on to train in Kathak for eight years; later on becoming a professionally trained Kathak dancer. Dixit made her cinema debut in 1984 with Rajshri Productions' drama Abodh, opposite Bengali actor, Tapas Paul. Upon release, the film failed commercially but Dixit's performance earned her positive reviews from critics too.

    Aishwariya Rai Bachchan - Indian Actress

    Aishwarya Rai Bachchan


    Aishwarya Rai Bachchan (born on 1st November 1973) is an Indian actress and the winner of the Miss World 1994 pageant. Primarily known for her work in Hindi films, she has established herself as one of the most popular and influential celebrities in India through her successful acting career. Rai has received numerous accolades, including two Filmfare Awards, and was honoured with the Padma Shri also. Her father, Krishnaraj, who died on 18 March 2017, was an Army biologist, while her mother, Vrinda, is a homemaker. She has one elder brother, Aditya Rai, who is an engineer in the merchant navy.

    Priyanka Chopra Jonas - Indian Actress

    Priyanka Chopra Jonas


    Priyanka Chopra Jonas is an Indian actress, singer, and film producer. The winner of the Miss World 2000 pageant, Chopra is one of India's highest-paid and most popular entertainers. She has received numerous accolades, including two National Film Award and five Filmfare Awards. After winning Miss India World, Chopra was cast as the female lead in Abbas-Mustan's romantic thriller Humraaz (2002), in which she was to make her film debut. However, this fell through for various reasons: she stated the production conflicted with her schedule, while the producers said they recast because Chopra took on various other commitments. She has made one to two Tamil debuts also.
